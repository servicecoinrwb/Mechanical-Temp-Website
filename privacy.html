Privacy Policy

Effective July 15, 2026

Mechanical Temp LLC ("Mechanical Temp," "we," "us") is a licensed HVAC and mechanical contractor serving residential and commercial customers in Michigan. This policy explains what information we collect when you request service from us, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

We collect only what we need to schedule, perform, and bill for the work you ask us to do:

  • Contact information — your name, phone number, email address, and service address.
  • Service details — the equipment involved, the problem you described, and notes, photos, and measurements our technician records on site.
  • Billing information — invoices, payments, and payment status. Card payments are processed by our payment provider; we do not store card numbers.

We collect this information directly from you when you call us, submit a form on our website, or contact us through Google Local Services Ads.

Text messaging

We do not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers or SMS consent with any third party for marketing purposes. Mobile information collected for text messaging is used only by Mechanical Temp to communicate with you about your service, and is never shared with third parties or affiliates for their own marketing.

Message frequency varies based on your service request — typically 1 to 5 messages per service visit. Message and data rates may apply.

How you consent

You consent to receive text messages from Mechanical Temp when you give us your phone number while requesting service — by calling us, submitting our website service request form, or contacting us through Google Local Services Ads.

What we send

Messages relate to the service you requested: appointment confirmations, arrival notifications, quotes, part status, payment links, and follow-up about the work performed. We do not send promotional or marketing text messages.

How to stop

Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. You will receive one confirmation message and no further texts. Reply START to resubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance, or call us at 313-282-4758.

How we use your information

  • To schedule and perform the service you requested.
  • To communicate with you about appointments, quotes, parts, and completed work.
  • To send invoices and process payments.
  • To keep service history for warranty and future repairs on your equipment.
  • To meet legal, licensing, tax, and insurance requirements.

How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only where necessary to do the work:

  • Service providers — our payment processor, scheduling and communication tools, and parts suppliers, limited to what each needs to perform its function.
  • Property managers or facility companies — when they are the party who requested and is paying for the work at your location.
  • Legal requirements — when required by law, subpoena, or to protect our rights.

As stated above, mobile phone numbers and SMS consent are never shared for marketing purposes.

Data retention

We keep service and billing records as long as needed for warranty coverage, future service on your equipment, and to meet tax and legal obligations — generally seven years. You may request deletion of information we are not required to keep.

Your choices

  • Reply STOP to any text message to stop receiving texts.
  • Ask us to correct or update your contact information.
  • Request a copy of the service records we hold for you.
  • Request deletion of information we are not legally required to retain.

To make any of these requests, contact us using the information below.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect your information, including access controls and encrypted transmission. No system is completely secure, but we limit who can access customer records to the people who need them to do the work.

Children

Our services are directed to property owners and managers. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the effective date above. Continued use of our services after a change means you accept the updated policy.
