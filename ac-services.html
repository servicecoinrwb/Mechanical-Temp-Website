Air Conditioning Services

Stay cool all summer with our expert AC repair, installation, and maintenance services in Southfield and Metro Detroit.

Your Complete Cooling Solution

Whether your AC has stopped working, needs a pre-season tune-up, or it's time for a high-efficiency upgrade, our team is here to help.

AC Repair

Is your AC blowing warm air, leaking water, or making unusual noises? Our technicians provide fast, 24/7 emergency AC repair for all brands. We'll find the issue and give you a clear, upfront price for the fix.

AC Installation

Upgrade to a new, high-efficiency air conditioner and save money on your summer energy bills. We'll help you select the perfect size and SEER rating for your home and provide a professional, clean installation for maximum comfort.

AC Maintenance

An annual AC tune-up is the best way to prevent unexpected breakdowns. Our maintenance service improves efficiency, extends the lifespan of your unit, and can catch small problems before they become expensive repairs.

We Fix All Common AC Problems

Don't suffer through a hot, humid Michigan summer. If your air conditioner is showing any of these signs, contact us for fast, reliable service.

  • ✓ AC is not turning on
  • ✓ Blowing warm or hot air
  • ✓ Water leaking from the indoor unit
  • ✓ Strange noises (buzzing, clanking, hissing)
  • ✓ Unit is constantly running
  • ✓ Ice or frost forming on the copper lines
