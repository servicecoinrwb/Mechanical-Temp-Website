About the Mechanical Temp Service Notifications SMS Program

Program name: Mechanical Temp Service Notifications

Description: Transactional text messages related to your HVAC service request only — appointment confirmations, technician arrival notifications, and service follow-ups. We do not send marketing or promotional texts through this program.

How you can opt in: (1) checking the SMS consent box on this page or on our service booking form, (2) verbally agreeing when you call us at (313) 282-4758 to request service, or (3) requesting service through Google Local Services and agreeing to be contacted about your appointment.

Message frequency: Varies with your service activity.

Cost: Message and data rates may apply per your mobile carrier plan.

Opt out: Reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for assistance, call (313) 282-4758, or email observer@mechanicaltemp.com.

Privacy: Phone numbers and SMS opt-in consent are never shared with or sold to third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. Full details in our Privacy Policy and SMS Terms & Conditions.
